Welcome to Patterns-Matter

Patterns Matter is a personal toy database of the author (Sayeed). This database highlights the quest for patterns—in Materials and in Music.

Explore materials datasets (pre/post-featurization) and enjoy original guitar/music clips. Downloads are provided to play with RDBMS tooling; please credit the original sources for raw data. Music here is for fun, non-commercial; redistribution is okay, but don’t reuse in your own compositions.

What you'll find here:

  • Materials Database: Property-centric datasets (band gap, formation energy, oxidation state, melting point, …) curated for reproducibility. Many are ML-ready. Implementation details live on the author’s GitHub.
  • Music & Guitar Clips: Selected performances/recordings you can preview and download for inspiration or personal listening.
